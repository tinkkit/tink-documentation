Bar

The bar component is a more modern alternative to the grid, especially useful when you need to guarantee proper responsive behaviour.

Basically it permits simple alignment for lists of items. You can choose to put your items in left-aligned, right-aligned or centered lists.

To guarantee complete compatibility with Internet Explorer 9 .bar-center must come last in your html code.
